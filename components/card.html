Characters

Anya Forger

Anya Forger (アーニャ・フォージャー, Ānya Fōjā?) is one of four main characters of SPY x FAMILY. Formerly known as "Subject 007" (被験体"007", Hikentai "007"?), she is a runaway child from an underground experiment which accidentally gave her the power to read people's thoughts. After her escape, she moved from place to place for shelter, including the orphanage where she tricked Loid Forger into adopting her.

Loid Forger

Loid Forger (ロイド・フォージャー, Roido Fōjā?) is the main protagonist of the SPY x FAMILY series. He has discarded everything about his past to work for his country as an undercover spy who acts under the codename "Twilight" (〈黄昏たそがれ〉, ?). For his current mission, he is required to marry and have a child, thus he enters a marriage with Yor Briar and becomes the adoptive father of Anya.

Yor Forger

Yor Forger (ヨル・フォージャー, Yoru Fōjā?), née Briar (ブライア, Buraia?), is one of four main characters of the SPY x FAMILY series. She is a 27-year-old woman who works as a normal clerk at Berlint City Hall, but is also an assassin who acts under the codename "Thorn Princess" (〈いばら姫ひめ〉, ?). She agrees in pretending to be Loid's wife and Anya's mother in order to help him reach his goals as well as her own.

Bond Forger

Bond Forger (ボンド・フォージャー, Bondo Fōjā?) is the pet of the Forger Family. Formerly known as Subject 8 (8号, 8 Gō?), Bond has been used for experiments that resulted in the dog gaining precognition abilities.

Yuri Briar

Yuri Briar (ユーリ・ブライア, Yūri Buraia?) is Yor Forger's younger brother and a twenty-year-old man who works in the State Security Service. He keeps his job at the SSS a secret from his sister and instead pretends to be a normal civil servant at the foreign ministry.

Fiona Frost

Fiona Frost (フィオナ・フロスト, Fiona Furosuto?) is a spy for WISE who acts under the codename "Nightfall" (〈夜帷とばり〉, ?). She works undercover as an assistant to Loid Forger at Berlint General Hospital to provide him with mission details.

Damian Desmond

Damian Desmond (ダミアン・デズモンド, Damian Dezumondo?) is the second son of Donovan Desmond and attends Eden Academy as part of Cecile Hall, Group 3.

Donovan Desmond

Donovan Desmond (ドノバン・デズモンド, Donoban Dezumondo?) is the President of the National Unity Party of Ostania and "Twilight"'s current target.

Becky Blackbell

Becky Blackbell (ベッキ・ブラックベル, Bekki Burakkuberu?) is the daughter of CEO Blackbell, a major military manufacturer. She attends Eden Academy as part of Cecile House, Group 3, and becomes Anya Forger's close friend.
